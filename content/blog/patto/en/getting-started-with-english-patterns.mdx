## What is Pattern Learning?

To speak English fluently, memorizing vocabulary isn't enough — you need to **internalize patterns** until they come out automatically. Expressions like "I've been ~ing" or "It turns out that ~" only feel natural after you've encountered them hundreds of times in context.

Patto is built to accelerate exactly that process.

## The Patto Learning Loop

1. **Read the Story** — Meet patterns naturally inside 100 short, engaging stories.
2. **Hide & Recall** — Cover the pattern and try to produce it from memory.
3. **Repeat 5 Times** — Practice each pattern across multiple sentences.
4. **Write & Get Reviewed** — Submit your own sentences for instant AI feedback.

## Why Stories?

Random example sentences don't stick. **Patterns learned inside a narrative** stay in memory far longer because the brain processes information more efficiently when there's context and emotion attached.
